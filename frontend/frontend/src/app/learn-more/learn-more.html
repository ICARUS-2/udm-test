Vector Shock

Vector Shock is an innovative Unity-based simulation game designed to transform the abstract principles of electricity and magnetism into a tangible, interactive experience for CEGEP and university-level physics students. By creating a dynamic virtual sandbox, the game allows learners to visualize and manipulate electric charges, field vectors, and magnetic forces in real-time, bridging the gap between theoretical equations and their practical manifestations. Students can experiment with constructing circuits, observing field line behavior, and witnessing the outcomes of electromagnetic interactions without the constraints of a physical lab. This hands-on approach not only reinforces core concepts like Gauss’s Law, Faraday’s Law, and Lorentz force but also fosters intuitive understanding through trial and error, making complex topics more accessible and engaging. Vector Shock stands as a powerful educational tool that empowers students to explore, discover, and master the fundamentals of electromagnetism in an immersive digital environment.
